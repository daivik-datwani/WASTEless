# WASTEless ♻️

> AI-powered waste sorting, gamified for Dubai — InnovAIte 2026

**🔗 Live App: [wasteless-ai.vercel.app](https://wasteless-ai.vercel.app)**

---

## The Problem

**77% of UAE waste goes to landfills.**

The UAE's recycling rate sits below 10% — not because people don't care, but because 
proper waste sorting is confusing, inconsistent, and unrewarding. Improper sorting at 
the source contaminates recyclables, overloads landfills, and undermines every 
downstream sustainability effort.

---

## The Solution

WASTEless is a gamified, AI-powered waste sorting app built for Dubai residents and 
businesses. Point your camera at any item — AI instantly identifies it and tells you 
exactly which Dubai bin it belongs in. Then it rewards you for sorting correctly.

**Scan. Sort. Earn. Repeat.**

---

## How It Works

1. **Scan** your waste pile with your camera
2. **AI analyzes** each item in real-time using Sonar (Perplexity AI)
3. **Follow** Dubai-specific sorting instructions
4. **Mark completed** as you sort each item
5. **Earn points** — 10 points per item sorted

---

## Features

- 📷 **AI Waste Scanner** — Camera-based instant waste classification powered by Sonar AI
- 🏆 **Gamification** — Points, daily streaks, and tier progression (Bronze → Silver → Gold)
- ⚡ **Energy Impact Tracking** — Real kWh saved calculated from your correct sorting
- 🥇 **Leaderboard** — Compete as Top Eco Warriors of Dubai (All Players / Top 10 / Friends)
- 📊 **Stats Dashboard** — Personal analytics on sorting history and impact
- 🕓 **History Log** — Full record of past scans and classifications
- 📍 **Drop-off Locations** — Find nearest Dubai recycling drop-off points
- 🇦🇪 **Dubai-Specific Rules** — Sorting logic built around Dubai 2026 bin standards:
  - 🟢 GREEN = Recyclables
  - 🟤 BROWN = Organic
  - ⚫ BLACK = General
  - 🔴 RED = Hazardous
