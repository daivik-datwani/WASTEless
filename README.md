# WASTEless ♻️

> AI-powered waste sorting, gamified for Dubai — InnovAIte 2026

Dubai's residents sort less than 20% of their waste correctly. The problem isn't awareness — 
it's friction. WASTEless removes that friction: point your camera at any item, and AI 
instantly tells you exactly which Dubai bin it belongs in (GREEN = recyclables, BROWN = 
organic, BLACK = general, RED = hazardous). Then it rewards you for it.

**Scan. Sort. Earn. Repeat.**

Built for the **Dubai Smart Waste Initiative** and aligned with **UAE Vision 2030** 
sustainability targets, WASTEless turns correct waste disposal into a competitive, 
trackable habit.

## How It Works

1. **Scan** your waste with your camera
2. **AI analyzes** each item instantly
3. **Follow** Dubai-specific sorting instructions
4. **Earn points** + track real energy saved (kWh)

---

## Features

- 📷 **AI Waste Scanner** — Camera-based instant waste classification
- 🏆 **Gamification** — Points, streaks, and tier progression (Bronze → Silver → Gold)
- ⚡ **Energy Impact Tracking** — See real kWh saved from your correct sorting
- 📊 **Stats Dashboard** — Personal analytics on sorting history and impact
- 🕓 **History Log** — Full record of past scans and classifications
- 📍 **Drop-off Locations** — Find nearest Dubai recycling drop-off points
- 🇦🇪 **Dubai-Specific Rules** — Sorting logic built around Dubai 2026 bin standards
